ABOUT US


LION's Fiotness Academy Founded in 2020 in a college's canteen where our directors taught many disciples about the method of training with the right methods and the art of changing lifestyle. Classic Fitness is India's most reputed fitness teaching training center for fitness learners. The aim is dedicated to building careers in fitness industry, we offer different courses, different schedule's because we want everyone to be able to make their dream their career. We are affiliated with American, European body's like ACE and EREPS and we are also the Skilling Partner for Jain University which allows us to not only give certifications but provide diploma's in the field of fitness.
